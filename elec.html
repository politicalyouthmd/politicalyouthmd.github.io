Frequenty Asked Questions:

  1. Register yourself to vote online or on paper. You may need to register as an absentee voter (look at requirements).
  2. Go to your precinct and bring some form of identification (whatever your precinct requires).
  3. Once all documents are completed, they will give you a ballot and you will move to a private voting booth.
  4. If using paper and pen ballots, completely fill in the oval next to the candidate's name, DO NOT mark outside of the oval.
  5. If your paper ballot uses a punching device, punch a small hole next to the name of the candidate you are voting for.
  6. Simply click your preferred candidates' names* using your finger or stylus if you are voting on a computer. For mechanical ballots, push a button next to the candidates' name.

    7. *If you do not wish to vote for every race on your ballot, you can leave some races blank.

  1. Be a citizen of the United States of America
  2. Be a resident of the state of Florida
  3. Be at least 18 years old
  4. Not have been found mentally incapacitated in any state with regards to voting without having said right restored
  5. Not have been convicted of a felony without having voting rights restored
